Importing:

Go to jquery.com, downloads, scroll down to url that looks like this: https://code.jquery.com/jquery-3.6.0.min.js and put it in script AT BOTTOM OF PAGE between closing body and html tags <script> src="https://code.jquery.com/jquery-3.6.0.min.js"></script> BEFORE your script imports.
